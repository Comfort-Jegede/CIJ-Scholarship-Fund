Eligibility requirement
  • Passport photograph with white background
  • Birth Certificate
  • School ID Card
  • Applicants must be an indigene of Ilupeju Ekiti
  • Local Government of Origin identification or Local Government Area Letter of Identification
  • WAEC/NECO O’level Result (Combination is acceptable)
  • JAMB Result | POLY-JAMB results | pre-degree final GPA (for students admitted without writing these entrance exams).
  • Admission Letter (from School or JAMB)
  • Applicants must have a minimum of 3.5/4 CGPA in Polytechnic or 4/5 CGPA in University.
  • Submission of the most current school transcript showing up to date CGPA
  • CV (Curriculum Vitae)/Resume (include an active record of youth leadership & volunteering within your community)
  • One page statement of purpose demonstrating a financial need & why the scholarship is important to your academic development.
  • Download & complete scholarship application form
  • Send completed application form & documents (in .pdf format) to CIJEducationFund@gmail.com
  • Scholarship will be awarded in the following fields: Medicine | Law | Engineering | Science | Arts & Social Science
